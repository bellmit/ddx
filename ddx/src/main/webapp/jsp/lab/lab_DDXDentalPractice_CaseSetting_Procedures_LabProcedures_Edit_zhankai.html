  • Turn Around by Attribute[hide]
  • You can optionally supply turn around days that are to be applied based on attribute selections. For example, a turn around rule could specify that if an anterior tooth is selected a different turn around day count should be applied.
    The attribute based turn around rule that offers the most attribute matches to the selection will be chosen.
    If no attribute based turn around rule apply, the above specified turn around day count will be used instead.
    If multiple attribute based turn around rules provide equal weight when matching a selection, the one with the greatest turn around will be chosen.
    Attribute based turn around rules replace, and do not add to, the above specified turn around day count.
